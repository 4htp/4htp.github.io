Michael Whitley
Category: Intenet of Things (IoT)
Date: Feb 1st, 2017

Michael Whitley, founder of EngeniusMicro presented on the Internet of Things (IoT). Michael Whitley is a licensed professional engineer and founder of EngeniusMicro, a small business focused on developing uncommon sensors that see beyond the ordinary to improve safety and increase value by finding failures before they happen. EngeniusMicro has 15 employees in the Huntsville and Atlanta areas and is located in the Times Building downtown, "above" Below the Radar. Michael holds 3 patents and has published over 30 peer-reviewed publications related to sensor technology.
